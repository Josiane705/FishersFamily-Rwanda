About Our Ministry

Fishers Family Rwanda is a dynamic gospel music ministry committed to uplifting hearts through spirit‑filled worship and praise. Born in the heart of Rwanda, our mission extends beyond music — we're building a community of faith, hope, and transformation.

Through concerts, worship nights, and community outreach, we create spaces where people encounter God's presence and experience spiritual renewal. Our vision is to see Rwanda and all of Africa transformed by the power of worship.
